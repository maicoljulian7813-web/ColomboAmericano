Introduction

Rovaniemi is a small, well-organized city with 65,000 residents. As the capital of Lapland, it serves as the central hub for Arctic tourism. The city offers a perfect blend of modern lifestyle and Sami culture. Its culture is based on eco-tourism and a deep respect for nature.

Population 65,000 residents
Region Capital of Lapland, Arctic hub
Culture Sami heritage & eco-tourism

Sports & Activities

What sports can I practice in this city?

01

Ice Hockey

People play ice hockey quickly on frozen lakes — one of the most traditional winter activities.

02

Snowboarding & Ice Skating

You can practice snowboarding or ice skating across the snowy landscapes of Lapland.

03

Sleigh Rides

You should drive sleighs carefully because they are very fast.

04

Santa Claus Village

Currently, many tourists are visiting Santa Claus Village to meet Santa. It is about 8 km from the city.

05

Aurora Borealis

You can see the Aurora Borealis at night, but you need to sit quietly to enjoy the experience.

Section 4 of 5

Gastronomy & Food

What kind of food should I try?

Main Dish

Poronkäristys

Reindeer stew with some butter and salt. It is a salty and delicious dish.

FAQ: How much butter? — It has a lot of butter.

Soup

Lohikeitto

A creamy salmon soup with many potatoes and carrots.

FAQ: Karelian cakes per serving? — Usually two cakes.

Drinks

Glögi & Kahvi

Glögi is a hot berry juice with cinnamon, ginger, and cardamom. Kahvi is Finland's traditional coffee.

Section 5 of 5

Frequently Asked Questions

When are the Northern Lights visible?

Usually between 9:00 PM and 2:00 AM.

Can I walk at night?

Yes, you can walk safely at night.

Should I bring warm, waterproof clothes?

Yes, because the temperature is about -15°C.

Can I pay with a contactless card?

Yes, you can use your card at markets, shops, and most activities.

Is the tap water drinkable?

Yes, it is among the cleanest in the world.

Are there supermarkets open late?

Yes, K-Citymarket is open until 10:00 PM.

How far is Santa Claus Village?

About 8 km — 15 minutes by bus.
