Useful Things To Know

Practical guidance before you go

Where is Rovaniemi?

Rovaniemi sits in Finnish Lapland on the Arctic Circle, making it one of the best-known gateways to northern Finland.

How do you get there?

Travelers commonly connect through Helsinki and continue by plane or overnight train for a more scenic route.

How do you move around?

The town is manageable, but Santa Claus Village and outdoor experiences are easier with local transport or organized tours.

What is the weather like?

Winter can be deeply cold, so high-quality layers are essential. Conditions also change quickly, which is why guided planning adds comfort.
